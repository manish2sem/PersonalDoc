Coverage under any of these plans may be subject to certain requirements or limitations. This information cannot be used to modify any coverage that is listed in separate insurance documents, policies, trust deeds, etc. In all cases, the provisions of these documents and policies and letter of offer (and subsequent changes) would apply. The company reserves the right to amend any compensation plan or information given in this section without prior notice. Please talk to Human Resources for further assistance.

Flexible Benefit Plan (FBP): Under Flexible Benefit Plan (FBP), you can choose the amount allocated to one or more of the following benefits each year (within the limit of your FBP) based on your personal needs. Alternatively you can take the entire amount as a cash allowance. FBP is subject to Income Tax and Fringe Benefit Tax as per prevailing Government rules and regulations.

- House Rent Allowance (HRA)
- Medical Expenses (other than medical insurance)
- Leave Travel Allowance (LTA)
- Car Plan (see details of the policy on Intranet)
- Additional purchase of Medical Insurance
- Conveyance Allowance
- Professional Development Allowance

Please note that the income tax treatment related to any of the elements of the FBP could change based on the emerging/changing legal provisions applicable.

Medical Insurance for Family: Aricent provides free comprehensive medical insurance benefit to the spouse and children of the employee. In addition, for the fiscal year 2009-2010, there is an attractive plan to cover the parents and parents-in-laws. As you can appreciate, this is a very big benefit since the commercial value of this is significant. Please see details in the Group Medical Insurance Policy.

Company Incentive Plan (CIP): The incentive payable is based on the details given in this year's Incentive Plan and is based on the performance against pre-defined goals (usually a combination of Company Performance, Functional Performance, Individual Performance).
• The incentive would be paid half yearly - based on April to September performance by October 31, and based on October to March performance by May 31. No minimum amount is guaranteed. This is an exciting mechanism to earn more based on performance.
• Incentive is payable only to those employees who are in active service on the last working day of the period, i.e., September 30 for the first half of the financial year and March 31 for the second half of the financial year. Employees who leave the services during either of the half-year periods will not be eligible to receive the incentive for that half year.
• For employees who join the Company mid-way in the year or quarter, their incentive payment would be prorated based on actual number of days worked in quarter or year.

Gratuity Scheme: The employee becomes eligible for gratuity benefits under the scheme after completion of one year of employment (if the period as mentioned here in is different from the period mentioned in your last compensation revision/offer letter, the period mentioned in your last compensation revision/offer letter shall be applicable). The gratuity is equivalent to 15 days of basic salary for each completed year of service (a month means 22 days for the purpose of calculating gratuity). Gratuity is payable on retirement from service or separation from the company or in the event of earlier death. In case of death while in service, the nominee will get gratuity calculated as though the employee had worked with the Company until the retirement age.

Company Lease: Aricent provides for company lease for renting of residential accommodation. The lease amount is adjusted against HRA as declared by an individual. Aricent would pay the security and advance (usually equivalent to six months rent) and recover the same from the salary as per the prevailing Company Policy.

Working Hours and Holidays: The normal working hours are between 8:30 a.m. to 5:30 p.m., Monday to Friday, with a 45-minute lunch break. There are 9 company holidays per year. The company provides 12 days Casual/Sick Leave , 15 days Privilege Leave and 1 day Floating Leave per year (on a pro rata basis depending on number of months worked in the company in a year). Holidays and weekends (or weekly offs) are not counted as leave. Aricent has a flexible working hours practice.
